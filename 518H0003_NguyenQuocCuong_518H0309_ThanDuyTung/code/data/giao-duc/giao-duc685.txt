I fall head over heels for her ().You are my love. I fall head over heels for you ().: Yêu từ cái nhìn đầu tiên (tình yêu sét đánh)Ví dụ: I love you at first sight ()Don’t ask why I pamper her. She is my love at first sight ().: Thích được ở bên cạnh ai đóVí dụ: Whenever I go out, I enjoy my boyfriend’s company ().I enjoy your company and take care of you ().: Có những lúc thăng trầmVí dụ: Our love has ups and downs but we never leave. ().Although our love has had ups and downs, we finally are hand in hand ().: Cảm nắng/say nắng/thích ai đóVí dụ: He is having a crush on the girl next room ().Don’t follow me. I have a crush on a boy in my group ().: Yêu nhau cho đến khi đầu bạc răng longVí dụ: We cherish each other till death tears us apart ().The couple promised to cherish each other till death tears us apart ().: Tận hưởng trọn vẹn khoảnh khắcVí dụ: When beside each other, we savor every moment of love ().The couples savor every moment when they still belong to each other ().: Mềm lòng vì ai đóVí dụ: I always have a soft spot for my boyfriend when he says sorry ().My father is very strict with anyone, but he always has a soft spot for my mother ().: Hẹn hò với ai đóVí dụ: Fall in love at first sight, we go on a date with each other at once ().He goes on date with me.
